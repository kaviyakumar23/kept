KEPT · ARCHITECTURE

How Kept turns a Slack promise into a verified closure

A customer promise made in Slack becomes a signed, sanitized closure — checked against live delivery evidence first. Built on Slack AI (Agent Builder) for the agent surface and MCP for proof collection. The model proposes; a pure engine decides; a human signs.
